<objective>
Reverse the Phase 20 D-13 soft-invalidate → manual-Recompute UX in favour of fully automatic
recompute (D-02): a SizeCard click recomputes immediately; a custom-size edit recomputes on a
clamp-guarded ~500ms debounce; a committed dimension change re-fits the canvas cleanly (the App
half of D-04, using the Plan 03 `isFitMode`). Delete every manual stale surface (page banner, rail
Recompute CTA, StepBar amber marker, forward-nav block) across App/RefineScreen/StepBar/AtelierShell,
retarget the coupled App.test.tsx tests in the same commit (P23 precedent), and cap the Refine rail
width (D-06). Reuse `handleRecomputeMatch`/`setMatchInputs` and the existing loading overlay — do not
rewrite the worker path.

Purpose: SC5 (auto-recompute on dimension change, no intermediate click; rail tightened),
SC8 (SizeCards do not jump the zoom — re-fit cleanly), SC4 (suite green after the retarget).
Output: An always-fresh Refine preview with no manual recompute affordance and a tighter rail.

D-04 trigger coverage (partial-by-design, WARNING-1 fold-in): this phase ships THREE of D-04's four
re-fit triggers — init + new-image (the existing step-2 `fitToContainer` effect) and grid-dimension
change (this plan's re-fit on a committed SizeCard / custom-size change). The FOURTH trigger,
**container-resize re-fit**, is intentionally DEFERRED to a fast-follow per RESEARCH Open-Q2 (a new
`ResizeObserver` risks the jsdom green suite). No must_have or verification in this phase asserts a
container-resize re-fit, so the partial delivery of locked decision D-04 is honest and visible in the
executable plan (3-of-4 triggers ship; 1 deferred).
</objective>
