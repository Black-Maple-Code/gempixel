## Visual Hierarchy & Focal Point

*   **Primary Focal Point:** The primary visual anchor of the Quote tab action card is the "Net Profit" / "Artist Profit" value (highlighted in Sky Blue font, size 24px, bold).
*   **Secondary Action Anchors:** The primary CTA buttons ("Order Canvas" and "Add Drills to Cart") are styled as secondary interaction anchors inside the card.
*   **Layout Hierarchy:** Consolidates pricing estimates first, followed by the net profit display, and ordering controls at the bottom of the card container.
