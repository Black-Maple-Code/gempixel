Ship the DATA-01 automated integrity test for the drill-variant table and surface unmapped colors at runtime. The test guards three properties: positive-integer SKUs, unique variant IDs (except an explicit allow-list of known shared pairs), and no empty reachable mapping for a palette color+shape (except an explicit allow-list of known holes) plus full palette coverage. Output: `variants.integrity.test.ts`, a `hasVariantMapping` predicate, allow-list constants with adjudication TODOs, App.tsx runtime surfacing, and a data-owner checkpoint.
</objective>
